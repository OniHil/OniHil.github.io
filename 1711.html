M-Building (Mechanical Engineering)

You've arrived at the M-Building, the home of Mechanical Engineering. This is where the physical world is engineered.
Within these walls, researchers explore fundamental questions: How does a material bend under stress?
How can heat be converted into useful work?
How do fluids flow over a surface?
But this field is no longer just about gears and engines.
Today, these core principles are merged with advanced electronics and intelligent control systems to create the field of mechatronics.
