Alfvénsalen

Your journey through the annals of science and engineering at KTH has led you here, to a place of honor: Alfvénsalen. This grand lecture hall is named after Hannes Alfvén, a titan of Swedish science and a Nobel laureate. His groundbreaking work in plasma physics fundamentally changed our understanding of the universe. This hall is more than just a room; it's a space where generations of students have been inspired and where new ideas are forged.
